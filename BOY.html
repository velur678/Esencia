BURNOUT
Burnout es el quinto álbum de BoyWithUke y el último proyecto bajo su nombre artístico. El primero sin su icónica máscara LED, el álbum fue precedido por los sencillos "Can You Feel It?", "Ghost" y "Gaslight". Desde 2021 realiza canciones principalmente con el ukelele, hasta Burnout, que no tiene ni una sola canción con el ukelele; Todas las canciones de Burnout tienen la guitarra como instrumento principal. Esto no significa que abandonarás el ukelele por completo, pero sí que ampliarás tu música mucho más. No se ha anunciado en este momento a qué cambiará su nombre, sin embargo, se dice que Burnout es su proyecto más personal hasta el momento. Ver pre-lanzamiento











MELATONIN DREAMS
“Melatonin Dreams” is BoyWithUke’s debut album, and was released on January 22, 2021. According to BoyWithUke, the album most likely deals with depression (as the actual theme was censored). Melatonin Dreams, according to BoyWithUke, is an album containing similar themes of depression, anxiety, and suicide, but “shows glimmers of hope in the darkness”. Ver Album











LUCID DREAMS
Lucid Dreams’’ is BoyWithUke’s fourth album and the final in the ‘’“Dreams’’ Saga,’’. tollowing his 2022 album Serotonin Dreams. It is set to released on October 6, 2023. The first single, “Rockstar”, was released on ‘’February 10, 2023 as part of the surprise EP Antisocial. The second single, “Trauma”, was released on August 4, 2023. The third and final single, “Migraine”, was released on September 22, 2023’. Ver Album











FEVER DREAMS
“Fever Dreams” contains 11 songs written, recorded and produced by BoyWithUke. They all were originally a “Minute Long Song” from his TikTok. Due to the growing popularity of them, he decided to fully produce and officially release them on June 4th, 2021. Ver Album
